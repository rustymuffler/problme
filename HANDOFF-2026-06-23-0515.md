3. **Research brief required.** No article may be drafted without `research-brief.md`.

5. **Proofreader must approve** before any content PR is opened.
6. **No fabricated quotes.** Every attributed quote must be real and verifiable with a source link.
7. **No unlicensed images.** Claude-generated, personal screenshots, or personal photos only.
8. **No secrets in source files.** Use environment variables and GitHub Secrets only.
9. **SEO checklist 100% complete** before any content PR opens.
10. **Lighthouse minimums:** Performance ≥ 90, Accessibility ≥ 95, SEO ≥ 95.
11. **No third-party scripts without review.** Exception already approved: the Umami tracking script from the project's own Vercel instance.
12. **Brand palette only.** No ad-hoc colors, fonts, or layouts. Everything follows BRAND.md.
13. **Only recommend tools we actually use.** Do not promote paid tools not in the probl.me or Celly workflow.
